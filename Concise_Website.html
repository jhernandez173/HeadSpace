Disparity: The UC SeeSaw

I'm not comparing UCSD to academic institutions of a different dichotomy, as to suggest a harrowing dichotomy such as the top Ivy-Leagues or elitist private schools. What I point to is the stalemate, longboard, flagship instution: UC Berkeley. Our sister school across the state wields opportuntity unlike its UC counterparts. The overwhelming benefit of location within the Bay Area is pivotal to student-industry pipeline. I cannot attend events, much less be offered the same recruiters. Stripe does not visit UCSD, neither does YCombinator or StartUp investors. UC Berkeley has their own start-up page within their FaceBook page! A testament to how indebted this culture pervades the institution. They consistantly hold the (A)best students, (B)best teachers, (C)best opportunities. If you thought this long-heard mantra of UCSD students being the lapless rejects of Bruin and Cal were driven by reputation of namesake alone, consider the factors our school doesn't offer and what it makes of our alumni.
